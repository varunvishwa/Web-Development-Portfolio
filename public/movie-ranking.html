The Best

These are the top ten best movies I have ever seen.

Interstellar

A perfect combination of emotion, sci-fi, thrill, love, suspense and oh my, that mesmerising soundtrack.

Shutter Island

Brillianty executed thriller with one of the best, if not the greatest plot twists of all time.

The Dark Knight

Hands down, the best Batman movie ever. A dark and emotionally rousing portrayal of the superhero, with Hans Zimmer's magic sprayed all over it.
